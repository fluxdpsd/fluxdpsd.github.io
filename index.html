About Us

Welcome to Flux! For us, physics isn't just a subject; it's an epic adventure! Institutionalized in 2016 by students at Delhi Public School Dwarka.

    Here curiosity meets exploration and the wonders of the universe come alive! Our club is a vibrant community of students who are passionate about understanding the fundamental principles that govern the natural world. Whether you're a seasoned physicist or just beginning to explore the mysteries of physics, there's a place for you here.
